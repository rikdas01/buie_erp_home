About

Established in 1998, Bankura Unnayani Institute of Engineering (BUIE) stands as a beacon of technical education in the economically backward region of Bankura, located just 4 km from the district town, at Pohabagan. Our institution was founded by the Bankura Unnayani Society, a registered body comprised of dedicated educationists and social activists, with the mission to disseminate technical education in the Jangal Mahal area, encompassing parts of Bankura, Purulia, Jhargram, West Midnapur, and neighboring states like Jharkhand, Bihar, and Orissa. BUIE is proud to have received the prestigious TEQIP-II project, an initiative of the World Bank under the MHRD, which has significantly enhanced our educational infrastructure.
